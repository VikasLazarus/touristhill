About

Discover the Magic of Kufri with Tourist Hill Tour and Travels

Nestled amidst the breathtaking Himalayan ranges, Kufri is a winter wonderland that promises an unforgettable adventure. With its pristine snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and vibrant culture, Kufri is a must-visit destination for every travel enthusiast.

At Tourist Hill Tour and Travels, we invite you to experience the thrill of Kufri like never before. Our carefully curated packages offer a perfect blend of adventure, nature, and culture.

Embark on an Unforgettable Horse Ride

One of the most enchanting experiences in Kufri is a leisurely horse ride through its picturesque landscapes. Imagine gliding through snow-covered trails, surrounded by the majestic Himalayas. Our expert guides will ensure your safety and comfort as you soak in the breathtaking views.

Adventure Awaits

For the adrenaline junkies, Kufri offers a plethora of adventure activities. From skiing and snowboarding to trekking and hiking, there's something for everyone. Our experienced team will provide you with top-notch equipment and guidance, ensuring an exhilarating experience.

  • Skiing and Snowboarding: Experience the thrill of gliding down the slopes, surrounded by snow-clad peaks.
  • Trekking and Hiking: Explore the hidden gems of Kufri through our guided trekking and hiking expeditions.
  • Other Adventures: Discover the joy of sledding, ice skating, and more!

Create Lasting Memories

Our commitment to providing exceptional service extends beyond adventure. We offer comfortable accommodations, delicious local cuisine, and cultural experiences to enrich your journey.

Book your Kufri adventure with Tourist Hill Tour and Travels today and get ready to create memories that will last a lifetime.
